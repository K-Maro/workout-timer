About Workout Timer

Workout Timer is a simple training companion for combat sports and fitness. It helps you manage rounds, rest periods, and total session time so you can focus on training â€” not the clock.

How to use

  • Set your round length, rest time, and number of rounds
  • Press Start Workout to begin
  • Follow the on-screen countdown and session timer

Features

  • 3-2-1 pre-start countdown
  • Customizable round & rest times
  • Pause, resume, or end at any time
  • Automatic total session time calculation
